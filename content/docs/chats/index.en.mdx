The **Chats** section is your command center for all AI agent conversations with customers. Here you observe conversations in real time, switch between AI and operator modes, send files and voice messages, and manage conversations.

## Key features

- **Two operating modes** — AI Mode (automated responses) and Operator Mode (a human agent replies manually)
- **Auto-switch** — automatic switching between modes based on configured triggers
- **Conversation list** with full conversation history, updated in real time
- **Filter by channel source** — WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, and other sources
- **File and image upload**, voice message support
- **Tagging system** for organizing conversations
- **Read/unread status** tracking
- **Message feedback** — like/dislike with correction prompts
- **Message search** and notification sound toggle
