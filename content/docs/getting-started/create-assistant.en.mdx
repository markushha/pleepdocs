---

Creating an AI agent takes just a few minutes. We'll walk you through each step.

<Callout title="Create your AI agent">
  [Create your AI agent →](https://app.pleep.app/create/wizard?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=create-assistant)
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Choose agent type

Decide what your AI agent will be used for:

- **Sales** — the agent will advise customers, help them choose, and guide them toward a purchase
- **Tech Support** — the agent will answer questions and help customers

### Add a data source

Paste your website URL or Instagram account link. Pleep will automatically scrape the page and extract your business information — products, services, prices, and other data.

<Callout title="Tip">
  You can add more data later through the Knowledge Base: upload files (PDF, DOCX, XLS, TXT), connect Google Docs/Sheets, add more websites, or write information manually.
</Callout>

### Set up branding

Customize the look of your AI agent:

- **Logo** — upload your company logo
- **Colors** — choose your brand colors
- **Company name** — enter the name your customers will see

### Configure persona

Define your AI agent's communication style — how it introduces itself, what tone it uses, and how it interacts with customers.

### Sales script generation

Based on the data Pleep extracted from your website or Instagram, the AI automatically generates an optimized sales script. The script includes scenarios for greeting, qualification, objection handling, and deal closing.

</Steps>

## What's next?

After creating your AI agent, go to [First Steps](/en/getting-started/first-steps) to test it and prepare it for real customer conversations.
