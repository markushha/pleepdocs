Pleep is a multimodal AI agent for sales. It qualifies leads, handles objections, and closes deals across messengers — 24/7, without human involvement.

<Callout title="Create your AI agent now">
  [Create your AI agent →](https://app.pleep.app/create/wizard?utm_source=docs&utm_medium=getting-started)
</Callout>

## How does it work?

1. **Paste a link to your website or Instagram** — Pleep automatically scrapes and extracts your business information
2. **Pleep analyzes the data and generates a sales script** — an optimized selling scenario based on your business
3. **Connect messengers — and the AI agent starts selling** — WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, or a website widget

## Key features

- [**It sells, not just chats**](https://pleep.app/features/chats) — lead qualification, objection handling, upselling, deal closing
- [**Messengers and integrations**](https://pleep.app/features/integrations) — WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, and a website widget from one unified inbox
- [**Voice Agent**](https://pleep.app/features/voice-agent) — AI-powered calls that qualify and follow up
- [**Knowledge Base**](https://pleep.app/features/knowledge-base) — accurate responses with no made-up information
- [**Improve on the fly**](https://pleep.app/features/improvement) — describe what needs to change, and the AI updates the script
- [**Broadcasts**](https://pleep.app/features/broadcasts) — text and voice outreach at scale
- [**Analytics**](https://pleep.app/features/analytics) — analysis of every conversation
