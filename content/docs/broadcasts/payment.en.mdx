title: Broadcast Billing
description: How WABA broadcast billing works — Meta charges directly, Pleep does not charge extra
---

When sending WABA broadcasts via the WhatsApp Business API, payment is charged by Meta directly. Pleep does not charge any additional fees for sending template messages.

## How does it work?

Meta sets the cost of each template message based on:

- **Recipient's country** — rates differ by country
- **Template category** — marketing messages cost more than utility messages
- **Sending volume** — Meta may provide free conversations within a certain limit

For Kazakhstan, the cost is approximately **30–40 KZT per recipient**.

## Setting up billing

To enable WABA broadcasts, you need to add a payment card in Meta's settings:

1. Go to Meta Business Settings
2. Open **Accounts** → **WhatsApp**
3. Select your business account
4. Navigate to **Payment Settings**
5. Click **Add payment method** and enter your card details

<Callout title="Broadcasts will not send without a card">
  If a payment method is not configured, WABA broadcasts will not be sent. Pleep will display an error when you attempt to send.
</Callout>

## Who charges you?

Meta charges your payment card directly. Pleep is not an intermediary in billing and does not take any additional commission for sending broadcasts.

## Free messages

If a customer messages you first, you can reply for free within **24 hours**. This applies to regular replies, not template broadcasts. Use this window to communicate with active customers at no additional cost.
