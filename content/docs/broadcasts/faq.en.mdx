## What broadcast types are available?

Pleep offers four types: **WhatsApp Broadcast** (direct sending), **WABA Broadcast** (via Meta-approved templates), **WABA Coexistence Broadcast** (hybrid AI agent and operator mode), and **Voice Broadcast** (voice calls via an AI agent).

## How much does it cost to send broadcasts?

For WABA broadcasts, the cost is set by Meta. For recipients in Kazakhstan, the price is approximately **30–40 KZT per message**. The exact cost depends on the template category (marketing, utility, authentication) and the recipient's country.

## When am I charged?

Payment is charged upon **message delivery** to the recipient. If a customer replies to your message, the subsequent conversation within the 24-hour window is free — you can respond at no additional cost.

## How do I set up billing?

To set up billing, go to **Meta Business Settings** → **Accounts** → **WhatsApp** → **Payment Settings** → **Add payment method** and enter your bank card details.

## What happens if I do not set up billing?

WABA broadcasts **will not be sent** until a payment card is added in Meta's settings. If you attempt to send a broadcast without billing configured, Pleep will display an error.

## Who charges me for broadcasts?

Meta charges your bank card directly. **Pleep does not charge any additional fees** for sending broadcasts — you only pay Meta for message delivery.

## Are there any free messages?

Yes. If a **customer messages you first**, you can reply for free within **24 hours**. This is known as the "service window" — within it, you can send regular (non-template) messages at no cost.

## Can I schedule a broadcast for a specific time?

Yes. When creating a broadcast, you can choose deferred sending — specify a date and time, and the broadcast will be sent automatically.

## What happens if a message fails to deliver?

Pleep automatically retries delivery on failure. You can track the status of each message in the broadcast analytics.

## What is the difference between WABA Coexistence and regular WABA?

In **WABA Coexistence** mode, incoming replies to the broadcast can be handled by either the AI agent or a live operator. This is useful when some conversations require human involvement.
