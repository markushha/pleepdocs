Pleep provides analytics tools to help you measure the effectiveness of your AI agent and understand how customers interact with your business.

## What you can track

Pleep analytics is available in the **Statistics** section of your application and includes:

- **Total messages processed** — how many messages your AI agent has handled over the selected period.
- **AI vs operator response split** — what share of responses comes from the AI agent versus live operators.
- **Message consumption over time** — how your message quota is being used over time.
- **Channel-wise analytics** — statistics for each connected channel (WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, etc.).
- **Chart visualization** — graphs and charts for a clear view of your metrics.

## Why analytics matter

Analytics help you make informed decisions:

- Assess the load on your AI agent and plan resources.
- Understand which channels generate the most inquiries.
- Monitor message consumption and purchase additional quota in time.
- Improve your AI agent's knowledge base using real data.
