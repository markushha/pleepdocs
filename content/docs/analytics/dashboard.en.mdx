The Pleep Dashboard is available in the **Statistics** section of your application and provides an overview of your AI agent's key performance metrics.

## Key metrics

### Total messages

The top section of the dashboard displays the total number of processed messages for the selected period. This is the primary indicator of your AI agent's workload.

### AI vs operator split

See what share of messages was handled by the AI agent versus live operators. This helps you evaluate automation effectiveness and understand when customers need operator assistance.

### Message consumption

The chart shows message consumption over time, allowing you to:

- Track workload trends.
- Plan additional message purchases.
- Identify peak inquiry periods.

### Channel-wise analytics

Statistics are broken down by each connected channel:

- **WhatsApp** — message and conversation counts.
- **Instagram** — incoming inquiries via Direct.
- **Telegram** — messages through the Telegram bot.
- **Other channels** — website widget and other connected channels.

## Filters

You can customize the data display:

- **Date range** — select a date range for analysis.
- **Granularity** — switch between hourly, daily, and monthly views.
- **Channel** — filter data by a specific messenger.

## Charts

All metrics are visualized as charts, allowing you to quickly assess trends and identify patterns.
