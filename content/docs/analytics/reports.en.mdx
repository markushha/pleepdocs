The reports section provides a more detailed analysis of your AI agent's performance with period selection and channel breakdowns.

## Available reports

### Channel breakdown

For each connected channel (WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, etc.), you can see:

- Number of processed messages.
- Share of AI responses versus operator responses.
- Inquiry trends over the selected period.

### Period report

Select a date range to view:

- **Hourly data** — for analyzing peak load hours.
- **Daily data** — for tracking day-by-day trends.
- **Monthly data** — for strategic planning.

### Message consumption

The report shows:

- Total message consumption for the period.
- Split between AI and operator.
- Remaining message quota.

## Google Analytics integration

You can connect Google Analytics for extended tracking:

1. Go to your application settings.
2. Find the **Google Analytics** section.
3. Enter your tracking identifier (Measurement ID).
4. Save your settings.

Once connected, AI agent interaction data will be sent to your Google Analytics dashboard, allowing you to combine chatbot analytics with your overall business analytics.
