title: Prerequisites
description: Complete list of requirements for connecting WhatsApp to Pleep via WhatsApp Business API
---

Before connecting WhatsApp to Pleep, make sure you have everything you need. Below is a detailed checklist with explanations for each item.

## 1. Phone number

You need a phone number that will be used for the WhatsApp Business API. There are two options:

### Option A: Use an existing number

If you already have a number with WhatsApp, you can use it to connect to Pleep via Coexistence. Thanks to Coexistence technology, **you do not need to delete your existing WhatsApp account** — the number will work simultaneously in WhatsApp Business App and through the API.

<Callout type="warn" title="If you are NOT using Coexistence">
  When connecting via the classic method (without Coexistence), you must **delete the existing WhatsApp account** on that number before registering with the API. All chats and data will be permanently lost. Pleep uses Coexistence by default, so deletion is not required in most cases.
</Callout>

### Option B: Buy a new number

If you want a dedicated number for business communications:

- Purchase a SIM card from any carrier
- The number must be able to receive SMS or calls for verification
- Virtual numbers (VoIP) are supported, but physical SIM cards are more reliable

## 2. Meta Business Portfolio (formerly Business Manager)

Meta Business Portfolio is the management center for your business within the Meta ecosystem (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp).

### How to create one

1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your company name, your name, and work email
4. Fill in your company details

<Callout type="warn" title="Warning: the name cannot be changed!">
  The company name in Meta Business Portfolio **cannot be changed** after creation. It must match the official name of your company and the name in your WhatsApp Business Profile. Double-check the spelling before creating it.
</Callout>

### Meta Business Portfolio requirements

- Must be linked to a real business (not a personal account)
- The email must be a working one — you will receive notifications from Meta
- It is recommended to assign at least two administrators for security

## 3. Facebook Page

A Facebook Page is required to link your WhatsApp Business Account to your Meta Business Portfolio.

### How to create one

1. Log in to Facebook
2. Click "Create" then "Page"
3. Select the type: **Business or Brand**
4. Fill in the name and category
5. Link the page to your Meta Business Portfolio

### Requirements

- The page must be of type "Business or Brand" (not "Community" or "Public Figure")
- The page name should correspond to your business
- The page must be linked to your Meta Business Portfolio

## 4. WhatsApp Business App

Before connecting to the API, your number must be registered in WhatsApp Business App and active.

### Number requirements

- WhatsApp Business App is installed on your phone
- The number is registered in WhatsApp Business App
- The account has been active for **at least 7 days** — this is important for stability
- The number regularly sends and receives messages (not a "dead" number)
- There are no Meta policy violations on this number

<Callout type="warn" title="The 7-day rule">
  If the number was registered in WhatsApp less than 7 days ago, the API connection may fail or the account may be blocked. Be sure to wait until the number is at least a week old.
</Callout>

## 5. Payment card in Meta Business Settings

Meta charges for template messages that you initiate. A payment card is required for broadcasts and templates to work.

### How to add one

1. Go to Meta Business Settings
2. Open the "Billing and Payments" section
3. Click "Add Payment Method"
4. Enter your bank card details (Visa or Mastercard)

### Pricing

- **Replies within 24 hours** of a customer's message — **free**
- **Template messages** (broadcasts, notifications) — charged at Meta's rates
- Cost depends on the recipient's country and message type
- For Kazakhstan, template messages cost approximately $0.03–0.05

<Callout title="Tip">
  Even if you do not plan to send broadcasts right away, add a card in advance. Without a linked card, sending template messages will be impossible.
</Callout>

## Final checklist

Before connecting, make sure you have:

- [ ] A phone number with active WhatsApp Business (7+ days)
- [ ] A Meta Business Portfolio at business.facebook.com
- [ ] A Facebook Page linked to the Business Portfolio
- [ ] A completed profile in WhatsApp Business App
- [ ] A payment card in Meta Business settings

When everything is ready, proceed to the [Connection Guide](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/connection-guide).
