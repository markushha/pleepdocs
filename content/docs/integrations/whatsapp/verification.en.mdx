title: Meta Verification
description: How to complete Meta business verification to remove WhatsApp Business API limits
---

Meta business verification is the process of confirming the legitimacy of your company. It is not mandatory to start using WhatsApp through Pleep, but it is strongly recommended for removing restrictions and increasing trust.

## Do I need verification?

### Without verification

- Connection to WhatsApp Business API is possible in **10 minutes**
- Limit: **250 unique contacts per day** for sending business-initiated messages
- All basic features are available: receiving messages, replies, AI agent
- Incoming messages from customers are unlimited — the limit applies only to outbound business-initiated messages

### With verification

- The limit increases in stages: **1,000 → 10,000 → 100,000 → unlimited**
- Limit increases happen automatically with a good quality rating
- Ability to obtain a "green checkmark" (Official Business Account) — an additional trust badge
- Higher priority when reviewing appeals

<Callout title="Recommendation">
  If you are just starting out and have a small customer base, you can connect without verification and complete it later. For businesses with more than 250 customers per day, verification is necessary.
</Callout>

## Verification process

<Steps>

### Step 1. Prepare your documents

You will need:

- **Company registration certificate** (or extract from the legal entities register)
- **Tax return** or tax registration certificate
- **Utility bill** or bank statement in the company's name (to confirm the address)
- Documents must be **in Latin script or with a notarized translation**

### Step 2. Review your profile

Before submitting the application, make sure:

- The name in Meta Business Portfolio matches the documents
- The company address is entered correctly
- Phone and email are filled in
- The company website is listed and working

### Step 3. Submit the application

1. Go to Meta Business Settings
2. In the left menu, open **Security Center**
3. Find the **Business verification** section
4. Click **Start Verification**
5. Select your country and document type
6. Upload your documents
7. Enter the verification code sent to the company's phone or email

### Step 4. Wait for the result

- Review typically takes **2–7 business days**
- In some cases, Meta may request additional documents
- You can track the status in Security Center
- The result will be sent to the email listed in Meta Business Portfolio

</Steps>

## Messaging limit tiers

After verification, limits increase automatically based on the quality rating:

| Tier | Unique contacts/day limit | Upgrade condition |
|------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Without verification | 250 | Complete verification |
| Tier 1 | 1,000 | Send 500+ messages in 7 days with a green rating |
| Tier 2 | 10,000 | Send 5,000+ messages in 7 days with a green rating |
| Tier 3 | 100,000 | Send 50,000+ messages in 7 days with a green rating |
| Unlimited | No limit | Automatically with a stable green rating at Tier 3 |

<Callout type="warn" title="Limit downgrades">
  Limits can be **downgraded** if the quality rating drops to red. Monitor your rating in WhatsApp Manager and respond to complaints promptly.
</Callout>

## Common reasons for rejection

- **Name mismatch** — the name in documents does not match Meta Business Portfolio
- **Unreadable documents** — poor quality scan or photo taken at an angle
- **Expired documents** — the certificate or statement has expired
- **Address mismatch** — the address in documents differs from the one in settings
- **Documents not in Latin script** — Meta does not accept documents in Cyrillic without translation

## Green checkmark (Official Business Account)

After successful verification, you can apply for a green checkmark — a verified business badge displayed next to your name in WhatsApp.

### Requirements

- Completed Meta business verification
- Sufficient brand recognition (assessed by Meta)
- Active and high-quality WhatsApp Business profile

### How to apply

1. Go to WhatsApp Manager
2. Open the number settings
3. Find the "Official Business Account" section
4. Submit the application

<Callout title="Note">
  The green checkmark is granted at Meta's discretion, and not all businesses receive it. It is not required for full WhatsApp Business API functionality.
</Callout>
