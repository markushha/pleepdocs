WhatsApp is the most popular messenger for business communication in Kazakhstan and Central Asia. Pleep's WhatsApp integration allows your AI agent to chat with customers directly in their favorite messenger.

## How the connection works

Pleep uses the **official WhatsApp Business API** via the **WhatsApp Coexistence** mechanism. This is the recommended and most stable connection method, approved by Meta.

When you connect, your existing WhatsApp number is linked to the Pleep platform. The AI agent gains the ability to receive and send messages on behalf of your business, while you retain full control over the number.

## Integration capabilities

- **Broadcasts** — send template messages to customers with news, promotions, and offers
- **Message templates** — use Meta-approved templates to initiate conversations with customers
- **Read receipts** — the AI agent can see when a customer has read a message
- **Typing indicators** — the customer sees that the agent is "typing" while a response is being generated
- **Media files** — send and receive images, documents, audio, and video
- **Interactive buttons** — quick reply buttons and lists for convenient navigation
- **Contacts and geolocation** — share contacts and locations

## What is WhatsApp Coexistence

WhatsApp Coexistence is a technology that allows you to use the same phone number simultaneously in WhatsApp Business App and through the WhatsApp Business API (Cloud API). This means you can:

- Continue using WhatsApp Business App on your phone for personal communication
- Simultaneously receive messages through Pleep for automatic processing by the AI agent
- Switch between manual and automatic message handling modes

<Callout title="Important">
  Connecting WhatsApp to Pleep requires a Meta Business Portfolio and a phone number with an active WhatsApp Business account. Read more about all requirements in the [Prerequisites](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/prerequisites) section.
</Callout>
