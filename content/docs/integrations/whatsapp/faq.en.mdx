## Connection

### Do I need Meta verification to connect WhatsApp?

No, verification is **not mandatory** for connection. You can connect WhatsApp to Pleep in 10 minutes without verification. However, without verification there is a limit of 250 unique contacts per day for outbound business-initiated messages.

Verification is recommended to remove limits, especially if you plan to send broadcasts. Read more in the [Meta Verification](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/verification) section.

### What are the phone number requirements?

Key requirements:

- The number must be registered in **WhatsApp Business App** (not regular WhatsApp)
- The account must be active for **at least 7 days**
- The number must be regularly used for sending and receiving messages
- There must be no Meta policy violations on the number

Thanks to Coexistence technology used by Pleep, **you do not need to delete your existing WhatsApp account**. The number will work simultaneously in the app and through the API.

If you want to use a new number, simply buy a SIM card, install WhatsApp Business, and wait 7 days before connecting to Pleep.

### How long does the connection take?

With all the necessary components ready (Meta Business Portfolio, Facebook Page, active number), the connection takes **10–15 minutes**. If you need to create accounts from scratch and wait 7 days for the number to be active, it will take correspondingly longer.

### Can I connect multiple WhatsApp numbers?

Yes, you can connect multiple WhatsApp numbers to a single Pleep account. Each number can be linked to a separate AI agent or handled by the same agent.

## Messages and limits

### What are the messaging limits?

Limits depend on verification status:

| Status | Unique contacts/day limit |
|--------|--------------------------|
| Without verification | 250 |
| Verified, Tier 1 | 1,000 |
| Tier 2 | 10,000 |
| Tier 3 | 100,000 |
| Unlimited | No limit |

The limit applies only to **outbound business-initiated messages** (templates, broadcasts). Replies to incoming customer messages within the 24-hour window are **unlimited**.

### What is the 24-hour window?

When a customer messages you on WhatsApp, a **24-hour service window** opens. During these 24 hours, you can send the customer any messages for free (not just templates).

After 24 hours without a response from the customer, you can reach them **only through a template message**, which is charged at Meta's rates.

### Why is a payment card needed?

Meta charges for **template messages** — these are messages you initiate first (broadcasts, notifications, reminders). The card is linked in Meta Business Settings to pay for these messages.

**Free:**
- Replies to customers within the 24-hour window
- Receiving incoming messages

**Paid:**
- Template messages (broadcasts, first contact with a customer)
- Cost for Kazakhstan: approximately $0.03–0.05 per message

Even if you do not plan to send broadcasts right away, we recommend adding a card in advance so the feature is available when needed.

## AI agent operation

### How does the AI agent handle WhatsApp messages?

When a customer sends a message to the connected WhatsApp number:

1. The message is received by Pleep through the WhatsApp Business API
2. The AI agent analyzes the message and conversation context
3. It generates a response based on the knowledge base and settings
4. It sends the reply to the customer on WhatsApp

The entire process takes a few seconds. The customer sees a typing indicator while the agent prepares the response.

### Can a human agent intervene in a conversation?

Yes. At any point, an agent can take over the conversation through the Pleep interface:

- Pause the AI agent for a specific chat
- Reply to the customer manually
- Return control to the AI agent

The AI agent can also automatically escalate complex questions to a human agent with a notification.

### Does the agent support media files?

Yes, the AI agent supports the following through WhatsApp:

- **Images** — sending and receiving product photos, instructions
- **Documents** — sending and receiving PDFs, price lists
- **Audio** — receiving voice messages from customers
- **Video** — sending and receiving video
- **Contacts** — exchanging contact cards
- **Geolocation** — sending and receiving locations

## Problems and solutions

### Customers are not receiving messages from the agent

Check:
1. Connection status in the "Integrations" section — should be green
2. Whether an AI agent is assigned to this integration
3. Whether the daily message limit has been exceeded
4. Quality rating in WhatsApp Manager — sending may be restricted with a red rating

### Number has been banned

A detailed guide on ban types and recovery methods is in the [Avoiding Bans](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/avoiding-bans) section.

### Error during connection

Most common causes:
- The number has been active for less than 7 days
- The number is already linked to another WhatsApp Business Account
- Meta Business Portfolio has not been created or configured
- Facebook Page is not linked to the Business Portfolio

Detailed troubleshooting instructions are in the [Connection Guide](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/connection-guide) section.
