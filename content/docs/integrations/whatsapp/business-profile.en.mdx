A properly configured WhatsApp Business Profile is not only your company's business card for customers, but also an important trust factor for Meta. Accounts with fully completed profiles receive fewer bans and have higher quality ratings.

## Why it matters

- **Customer trust** — a professional profile inspires more confidence, and customers are more willing to engage
- **Meta trust** — a completed profile signals to Meta that this is a legitimate business
- **Reduced ban risk** — accounts with empty profiles are more frequently flagged by automated checks
- **Verification** — to pass Meta verification, the profile must be fully completed

## Required profile fields

Fill in **all** fields in WhatsApp Business App, then Settings, then Company Profile:

### Display Name

This is the name that customers see in the chat. It must strictly comply with Meta's rules.

**Display name rules:**

- Must match the legal company name or a recognizable trade name
- Must match the name on your website and social media
- Must not consist of only generic words (e.g., "Store" or "Delivery")

**What is prohibited:**

- Emojis in the name
- Writing entirely in ALL CAPS
- Geographic indicators without brand context (e.g., just "Astana")
- Generic words without a brand name
- Slogans and advertising phrases

<Callout type="warn" title="The name must match everywhere">
  The company name in your **Meta Business Portfolio**, on your **Facebook Page**, and in your **WhatsApp Business Profile** must all match. Name discrepancies are one of the most common reasons for verification rejection and account bans.
</Callout>

### Name examples

| Bad | Good | Why |
|-----|------|-----|
| Pleep ❤️ | Pleep | Emojis are prohibited |
| PLEEP | Pleep | Cannot write everything in caps |
| Astana | Pleep Almaty | Cannot use only geographic names |
| Best Store | Pleep Store | Cannot use generic words without a brand |
| Pleep — AI for Business | Pleep | Slogans are prohibited |
| Pleep Support Department | Pleep Support Department | Acceptable if it is a separate department |

### About (Description)

- Maximum **139 characters**
- Briefly describe what your business does
- Example: "AI agents for sales and customer support in messengers"

### Business category

Choose the most appropriate category from the WhatsApp list. This helps customers understand the type of your business.

### Address

Enter the physical address of your office or retail location. If you do not have a physical address (e.g., online business), use the company's legal address.

### Business hours

Enter the actual working hours of your support team. If the AI agent operates 24/7, you can set round-the-clock hours.

### Contact information

- **Website** — enter your website URL (must include https://)
- **Email** — a working email for customer inquiries

## Profile photo

The profile photo is the first thing a customer sees. Use:

- Your **company logo** in high resolution
- Minimum size: **640x640 pixels**
- Format: square image
- Background: high contrast so the logo is clearly visible even at small sizes
- Do not use text in the photo — it will be unreadable at small sizes

<Callout title="Recommendation">
  Use the same logo as on your website and social media. This increases brand recognition and customer trust.
</Callout>

## Profile review checklist

After completing the profile, verify:

1. **Name** matches your Meta Business Portfolio and Facebook Page
2. **Description** is filled in and informative
3. **All fields** are completed without gaps
4. **Photo** is a high-quality logo in the correct format
5. **Website** URL is working and leads to your actual company website
6. **Business hours** are set correctly

## Common mistakes

- **Different names** in Meta Business Portfolio and WhatsApp — leads to verification rejection
- **Empty profile** — increases the risk of bans when sending broadcasts
- **Description too long** — gets truncated and looks unprofessional
- **Low-quality photo** — a blurry logo reduces customer trust
- **Non-existent website** — Meta may consider this as fraud
