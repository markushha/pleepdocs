title: Connection Guide
description: Detailed step-by-step instructions for connecting WhatsApp to the Pleep platform via WhatsApp Business API
---

Follow this guide to connect WhatsApp to Pleep. The entire process takes 10–15 minutes if you have already prepared all the [prerequisites](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/prerequisites).

<Callout type="warn" title="Before you begin">
  Make sure your phone number has been registered in WhatsApp Business App for **at least 7 days**, is regularly used for sending and receiving messages, and has no Meta policy violations. Failure to meet these conditions may result in your number being blocked.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Step 1. Install WhatsApp Business App and register your number

If you have not done this yet:

1. Download **WhatsApp Business** from the App Store or Google Play (this is a separate app from regular WhatsApp)
2. Open the app and register your business number
3. Verify the number via SMS code
4. Fill in the company profile:
   - Company name
   - Business category
   - Business description
   - Address
   - Business hours
   - Website URL
   - Email

<Callout title="Tip">
  Fill in the profile as completely as possible — this increases Meta's trust in your account and reduces the risk of bans. Read more about profile setup in the [WhatsApp Business Profile](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/business-profile) section.
</Callout>

### Step 2. Create a Meta Business Portfolio

1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Log in with your Facebook account (use the personal account of the business owner or administrator)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Enter:
   - **Company name** — must exactly match the official name (e.g., "Pleep LLC", not "pleep" or "PLEEP")
   - **Your name** — the administrator's name
   - **Work email** — you will receive notifications here
5. Click "Submit"
6. Confirm your email by clicking the link in the email

<Callout type="warn" title="Warning">
  The company name in Meta Business Portfolio **cannot be changed** after creation. Make sure it exactly matches the name on your website, documents, and WhatsApp Business Profile.
</Callout>

### Step 3. Create a Facebook Page

If your company does not have a Facebook Page yet:

1. Log in to Facebook
2. In the left menu, click **"Pages"** then **"Create New Page"**
3. Select the type: **Business or Brand**
4. Enter the page name (should match the company name)
5. Choose a business category
6. Add a logo and cover photo
7. Link the page to your Meta Business Portfolio:
   - Go to Meta Business Settings
   - Open "Accounts" then "Pages"
   - Click "Add" then "Add a Page"
   - Enter the name or URL of your page

### Step 4. Connect WhatsApp in Pleep

1. Log in to your Pleep dashboard
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in the sidebar
3. Find the **WhatsApp** card and click **"Connect"**
4. A Meta authorization window (Embedded Signup) will open
5. Log in with the Facebook account that is an administrator of the Meta Business Portfolio
6. Select your **Meta Business Portfolio** from the list
7. Select or create a **WhatsApp Business Account**
8. Choose the **phone number** you want to connect
9. Confirm the number (if required) via SMS code or phone call
10. Click **"Finish"**

### Step 5. Wait for verification

After completing the connection process:

1. Pleep will verify the connection to the WhatsApp Business API
2. You will see the connection status in the "Integrations" section
3. Status indicators:
   - **Connected** (green) — everything is working, you can start
   - **Pending verification** (yellow) — Meta is reviewing your account, usually takes up to 10 minutes
   - **Error** (red) — something went wrong, check the error description

</Steps>

## After connecting

Once WhatsApp is successfully connected:

1. **Assign an agent** — go to the integration settings and select the AI agent that will handle incoming messages
2. **Test it** — send a message to the connected number from another phone and make sure the agent responds
3. **Set up a welcome message** — in the agent settings, you can configure an automatic greeting for new customers
4. **Configure templates** — if you plan to send broadcasts, create templates and submit them for Meta approval

## Troubleshooting

### "Number already registered" error

The number is already linked to another WhatsApp Business Account. You need to unlink it from the previous account in Meta Business Settings first.

### "Business verification required" error

Meta requires business verification. Go to Meta Business Settings, then Security Center, and complete the verification process. Read more in the [Meta Verification](/docs/integrations/whatsapp/verification) section.

### SMS code not arriving

- Make sure the number can receive SMS
- Try the phone call verification option
- Check whether your carrier blocks SMS from international senders
