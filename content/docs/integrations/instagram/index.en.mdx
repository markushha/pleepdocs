The Instagram integration allows your Pleep AI agent to automatically respond to incoming messages in Instagram Direct. Customers message you on Instagram — the AI instantly replies, provides advice, and helps with product or service selection.

## How does it work?

Once Instagram is connected to Pleep, all incoming Direct messages are automatically handled by the AI agent:

1. **A customer sends a message** to your Instagram account's Direct
2. **Pleep receives the message** via the official Instagram Messaging API
3. **The AI agent analyzes the request**, consults your knowledge base, and generates a response
4. **The response is sent to the customer** directly in their DMs — quickly and naturally

The AI agent works around the clock, so your customers receive responses even at night, on weekends, and during holidays.

## What is supported?

- **Text messages** — the AI reads and responds to text messages in Direct
- **Quick replies** — the AI can use quick reply buttons to simplify conversations
- **Automatic greetings** — configure a welcome message for new customers
- **Handoff to a live agent** — if the AI cannot answer, the conversation is transferred to a live agent with a notification

<Callout type="warn" title="Business accounts only">
  This integration works **only with Instagram Business accounts**. Personal accounts are not supported. If you have a personal account, switch to a Business account before connecting. Learn more in [Prerequisites](/docs/integrations/instagram/prerequisites).
</Callout>

## Benefits of the integration

- **Instant responses** — customers do not wait for an agent to become available
- **24/7 availability** — the AI responds around the clock without breaks
- **Single dashboard** — all Instagram conversations are available in the Pleep panel alongside WhatsApp and Telegram
- **Accurate answers** — the AI uses your knowledge base and responds based on real information about your business
- **Conversation history** — all dialogues are saved and available for analysis
