Follow this step-by-step guide to connect Instagram Direct Messages to your AI agent in Pleep. The entire process takes 5-10 minutes.

<Callout type="warn" title="Before you begin">
  Make sure you have completed all the [prerequisites](/docs/integrations/instagram/prerequisites): an Instagram Business account, a linked Facebook Page, admin access, and messaging access enabled.
</Callout>

## Step-by-step instructions

<Steps>

### Step 1. Switch Instagram to a Business account

If your account is already a Business account, skip this step.

1. Open the **Instagram** app on your phone
2. Go to your **profile** (icon in the bottom right corner)
3. Tap the **menu** (three lines) in the top right corner
4. Select **Settings and privacy**
5. Tap **Account type and tools**
6. Select **Switch to Professional account**
7. Choose the category that best describes your business
8. Select the **Business** type
9. Fill in the contact information and complete the setup

<Callout title="Note">
  After switching to a Business account, it may take a few minutes before all features become available. If the next steps do not work, wait 5-10 minutes.
</Callout>

### Step 2. Link Instagram to a Facebook Page

If Instagram is already linked to a Facebook Page, skip this step.

1. In Instagram, go to **Settings and privacy**
2. Select **Business** or **Account**
3. Tap **Linked accounts** or **Accounts Center**
4. Select **Facebook** and log into your Facebook account
5. Choose the Facebook Page you want to link to Instagram
6. Confirm the link

If you do not have a Facebook Page yet, create one at facebook.com/pages/create and return to this step.

### Step 3. Enable messaging access in Instagram

1. In Instagram, go to **Settings and privacy**
2. Select **Messages and story replies**
3. Tap **Message controls**
4. Under **Connected tools**, enable **Allow access to messages**

Without this setting, Pleep will not be able to receive and send messages on behalf of your account.

### Step 4. Connect Instagram in Pleep

1. Open the **Pleep** dashboard in your browser
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in the side menu
3. Find the **Instagram** card and click **Connect**
4. Select the AI agent that will respond to Instagram messages

### Step 5. Authorize through Facebook

1. A Facebook authorization window will open
2. Log into the Facebook account that is the **admin** of the page
3. Select the **Facebook Page** you want to connect
4. Select the **Instagram account** linked to that page
5. Review the requested permissions and click **Allow**

<Callout type="warn" title="All permissions are required">
  Do not uncheck any permissions during authorization. If you deny even one permission, the integration will not work correctly. You will need to disconnect and reconnect Instagram.
</Callout>

### Step 6. Wait for automatic connection

After authorization, Pleep will automatically:

- Obtain an access token for your Instagram account
- Set up automatic connection for receiving new messages
- Verify the connection and check the status

This process takes a few seconds. Once complete, the integration status will change to **Connected** (green indicator).

### Step 7. Send a test message

1. Open Instagram from **a different account** (or ask someone to help)
2. Find your business account on Instagram
3. Send a Direct message — for example, "Hi, tell me about your products"
4. Verify that the AI agent **responded** to the message
5. Check that the conversation appears in the **Chats** section of the Pleep dashboard

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### The Facebook authorization window does not appear

- Make sure pop-ups are not blocked in your browser
- Try disabling ad-blocking extensions
- Try a different browser

### The AI agent does not respond to the test message

- Check that the integration status in Pleep is **Connected**
- Verify that you enabled messaging access in Instagram settings (Step 3)
- Check that the AI agent is active and has a loaded knowledge base
- Wait 1-2 minutes — sometimes the first message is processed with a slight delay

### "Insufficient permissions" error

- Make sure you authorized through the Facebook account that is the page admin
- Verify that you did not uncheck any permissions during authorization

<Callout title="Connection complete!">
  Congratulations! Your AI agent now automatically responds to messages in Instagram Direct. All conversations are available in the **Chats** section of the Pleep dashboard.
</Callout>
