title: Prerequisites
description: Requirements and prerequisites for connecting Instagram Direct Messages to the Pleep platform
---

Before connecting Instagram to Pleep, make sure you have everything you need. The integration works through the official Instagram Messaging API, which requires specific account configuration.

## Requirements

### 1. Instagram Business account

<Callout type="warn" title="Personal accounts are not supported">
  The Pleep integration works **only** with Instagram Business or Instagram Creator accounts. If you have a personal account, you must switch to a Professional account **before** starting the setup. Without this, Instagram will not provide access to the Messaging API.
</Callout>

To check your account type:

1. Open Instagram and go to your profile
2. Tap the **menu** (three lines) in the top right corner
3. Go to **Settings and privacy**
4. Select **Account type and tools**
5. If you see the option "Switch to Professional account" — you have a personal account and need to switch

### 2. Facebook Page connected to Instagram

Your Instagram Business account must be **linked to a Facebook Page**. This is a mandatory requirement of the Instagram Messaging API.

If you do not have a Facebook Page yet:

- Create a page at facebook.com/pages/create
- The page name should match your business
- Fill in the basic information: category, description, contacts

### 3. Admin access to the Facebook Page

You must be an **administrator** of the Facebook Page that is linked to your Instagram account. Without admin access, you will not be able to grant Pleep the necessary permissions.

To check your role:

1. Open your Facebook Page
2. Go to the Page **Settings**
3. Select **Page Roles**
4. Verify that your account is listed as **Admin**

<Callout type="warn" title="No admin access?">
  If you are not an admin of the page, ask the current admin either to grant you the admin role or to set up the integration in Pleep themselves.
</Callout>

### 4. Facebook App permissions for Instagram Messaging

When connecting Instagram through Pleep, the system will request the following permissions:

- **instagram_manage_messages** — read and send messages in Direct
- **instagram_basic** — access to basic profile information
- **pages_manage_metadata** — manage page notification subscriptions
- **pages_messaging** — receive and send messages through the page

All of these permissions are requested automatically during the setup process. You simply need to **confirm access** when authorizing through Facebook.

## Pre-connection checklist

Make sure all items are complete:

- [ ] You have an Instagram Business or Creator account (not personal)
- [ ] Instagram is linked to a Facebook Page
- [ ] You are an administrator of that Facebook Page
- [ ] You know the login credentials for the Facebook account that is the page admin
- [ ] Your Instagram account has messaging access enabled for connected apps

## Enabling messaging access

Before connecting, make sure messaging access for third-party apps is enabled in Instagram:

1. Open Instagram and go to **Settings and privacy**
2. Select **Messages and story replies**
3. Select **Message controls**
4. Under **Connected tools**, enable the **Allow access to messages** setting

<Callout title="All set?">
  If all checklist items are complete, proceed to the [Connection Guide](/docs/integrations/instagram/connection-guide).
</Callout>
