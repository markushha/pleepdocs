---
title: Disconnecting Instagram
description: How to disconnect the Instagram integration from Pleep and what happens to existing conversations
---

If you need to disconnect the Instagram integration from Pleep, follow the instructions below. You can disconnect the integration at any time and reconnect it when needed.

## How to disconnect Instagram

<Steps>

### Step 1. Open integration settings

1. Log into the **Pleep** dashboard
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in the side menu
3. Find the **Instagram** card with the **Connected** status

### Step 2. Disconnect the integration

1. Click on the Instagram card or the **Settings** button (gear icon)
2. In the window that opens, click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the disconnection in the dialog box

### Step 3. Verify the status

After disconnecting:

- The integration status will change to **Not connected**
- The AI agent will **stop** responding to new messages in Instagram Direct
- Automatic notifications will be disabled — Pleep will no longer receive notifications about incoming messages

</Steps>

## What happens to existing conversations

<Callout title="Data is preserved">
  All existing conversations **remain in the chat history** of Pleep. You can view them in the **Chats** section after disconnecting the integration.
</Callout>

After disconnecting Instagram:

- **Conversations are preserved** — the entire dialogue history with customers remains accessible in the Pleep dashboard for viewing and analysis
- **The AI stops responding** — new messages in Instagram Direct will not be processed by the AI agent
- **Customers are not notified** — customers will not be informed about the disconnection; their messages will simply go without an automatic response
- **The access token is deleted** — Pleep deletes the stored authorization token; when reconnecting, you will need to authorize through Facebook again

## Revoking permissions in Facebook

If you want to completely revoke Pleep's access to your Instagram, in addition to disconnecting in Pleep, you can remove the app from Facebook:

1. Open **Facebook** and go to **Settings and privacy** then **Settings**
2. Select **Security and login** then **Apps and Websites**
3. Find **Pleep** in the list of active apps
4. Click **Remove** and confirm the action

<Callout type="warn" title="After removing the app">
  If you remove the Pleep app from Facebook, reconnecting will require going through the full authorization process again and granting all permissions.
</Callout>

## How to reconnect Instagram

If you decide to reconnect Instagram to Pleep:

1. Make sure all the [prerequisites](/docs/integrations/instagram/prerequisites) are still met
2. Navigate to **Integrations** in Pleep
3. Click **Connect** on the Instagram card
4. Complete the Facebook authorization again
5. Send a test message to verify

All previous conversations will remain in the history. New messages from customers will start being processed by the AI agent immediately after reconnecting.

## Temporary pause

If you need to temporarily pause message processing without fully disconnecting the integration:

- In **Integrations** then **Instagram** then **Settings**, switch the AI agent to **Pause** mode
- In pause mode, incoming messages will still arrive in Pleep, but the AI will not respond automatically
- Agents can respond manually through the Pleep dashboard
- To resume AI operation, disable pause mode

<Callout title="Need help?">
  If you encounter issues with disconnecting or reconnecting Instagram, contact Pleep support through the chat in the dashboard.
</Callout>
