---
title: Connecting Telegram
description: Step-by-step guide to connecting a Telegram bot to Pleep.
---

Follow these steps to connect a Telegram bot to your Pleep account.

## Step 1. Create a Bot via @BotFather

1. Open Telegram and search for [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather).
2. Send the `/newbot` command.
3. Follow the instructions: provide a name and username for your bot.
4. After creation, BotFather will send you a **bot token** — a string like `123456:ABC-DEF1234ghIkl-zyx57W2v1u123ew11`.

## Step 2. Copy the Bot Token

Copy the token that BotFather sent you. You will need it in the next step.

> Do not share the bot token with anyone. The token grants full access to manage the bot.

## Step 3. Connect the Bot in Pleep

1. Log in to the Pleep dashboard.
2. Go to **Integrations** → **Telegram** → **Connect**.
3. Paste the bot token into the corresponding field.
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 4. Test the Connection

1. Open Telegram and find your bot by its username.
2. Send a test message to the bot.
3. Verify that the AI agent responds to the message.

If the bot does not respond, double-check the token and try reconnecting.
