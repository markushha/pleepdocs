---
title: Working Hours
description: Configuring the AI agent's operating schedule by day of the week
---

The **Working Hours** extension on the `/extensions` page lets you set the AI agent's operating schedule. Outside working hours, the agent's behavior changes automatically.

## Why configure working hours?

Working hours determine when the AI agent is active and responds to customers in standard mode. Outside working hours, the agent can switch to different behavior — for example, letting the customer know it is currently off-hours.

## Setting up the schedule

In the Working Hours extension, you set the schedule by day of the week:

- For each day, specify the **start time** and **end time** of the working day
- Mark days as **days off** if the AI agent should not operate in standard mode
- The timezone is taken from agent settings (`/settings`)

**Example schedule:**
- Monday through Friday: 09:00 — 20:00
- Saturday: 10:00 — 16:00
- Sunday: day off

## Behavior outside working hours

When working hours end, the AI agent automatically switches its behavior. You can configure what happens:

- Send an automatic off-hours message
- Switch to Operator Mode
- Other actions defined in agent settings

New customer messages are saved and will be available in the monitoring section.

<Callout title="Tip">
  If your business operates 24/7, you do not need to enable this extension — the AI agent will respond at any time.
</Callout>
