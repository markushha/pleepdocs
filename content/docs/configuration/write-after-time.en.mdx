---
title: Write After Time
description: Automatic messages to customers after inactivity (PRO feature)
---

The **Write After Time** extension on the `/extensions` page lets the AI agent automatically send a message to a customer who has not responded within a specified time. This feature is available on the **PRO** plan.

## How it works

1. The AI agent sends a message to the customer
2. The customer does not respond within the specified time
3. The AI agent automatically sends a follow-up message
4. If the customer responds at any point, the automatic chain stops

## Why use it

- **Win back customers** — many buyers get distracted and forget to respond. A reminder brings them back into the conversation
- **Increase conversion** — an additional touchpoint improves the chances of closing a deal
- **Automation** — no need to manually track who has not responded

## Configuration

### Wait time

Specify how long the AI agent should wait before sending a follow-up if the customer has not responded:

- After 30 minutes
- After 1 hour
- After several hours
- After 1 day
- Custom time

### Message text

The AI agent can:

- Send a predefined text
- Generate a contextual message based on the previous conversation

### Number of retries

Define how many times the AI agent can send follow-up messages. We recommend limiting it to 1—2 to avoid being intrusive.

## Example

> **AI agent:** "Hello! Are you interested in model X in white? It is currently in stock."
>
> *Customer does not respond for 2 hours*
>
> **AI agent:** "I wanted to check — are you still considering model X? We would be happy to help you with your choice."

<Callout title="Important">
  This feature is available only on the PRO plan. Use it carefully — one or two reminders is optimal. Too many follow-up messages can irritate customers.
</Callout>
