title: Scrape Instagram

---

If you actively maintain an Instagram presence, its content is an excellent source of information for your AI agent. Pleep can automatically scrape an Instagram account, and AI will filter and structure the relevant business information.

## How It Works

Pleep downloads content from the specified Instagram profile, then AI automatically analyzes the posts, filters out irrelevant content, and structures useful business information for the knowledge base.

## How to Start Scraping

<Steps>

### Open the Knowledge Base

Go to the **Knowledge Base** section in your agent's dashboard.

### Select "Instagram" as the source

Click on **Instagram** among the available data sources.

### Enter the Instagram profile

Provide the username (handle) or profile URL of the Instagram account you want to scrape.

### Start the scrape

Click the start button. The system will automatically download content from the profile.

### Wait for processing

AI will analyze the posts, filter out irrelevant content, and add structured information to the knowledge base. Track the processing status on the knowledge base page.

</Steps>

## When Is This Useful

- You sell products through Instagram and describe them in posts
- You publish information about services, promotions, and new arrivals
- Your customers frequently ask questions that are already answered in your posts

<Callout title="AI Filtering">
  Pleep does not simply copy post text — AI automatically determines which information from your posts is useful for business and adds only relevant content to the knowledge base.
</Callout>
