---
title: Manual Entry
description: How to add information to the knowledge base manually
---

Manual entry lets you add information to the knowledge base that is not available in documents, on your website, or on Instagram. This is especially useful for precise answers to common questions and for internal team knowledge.

## Two Entry Formats

### Q&A Pairs

Create precise question-and-answer pairs. When a customer asks a similar question, the agent will give exactly the answer you specified.

**Example:**

> **Question:** What payment methods do you accept?
>
> **Answer:** We accept cash, bank card, Kaspi transfer, and Kaspi QR payments.

### Free-form Text

Write information in any format. For example, a return policy description, delivery terms, or specifics of a particular service.

## How to Add an Entry

<Steps>

### Open the Knowledge Base

Go to the **Knowledge Base** section in your agent's dashboard.

### Select manual entry

Click on **Manual Entry** among the available data sources.

### Choose the format

Select the entry format: **Q&A** (question-answer pair) or **free-form text**.

### Enter the content

Fill in the fields — question and answer for Q&A format, or text for free-form format.

### Save the entry

Click the save button. The entry will be processed and added to the knowledge base.

</Steps>

## When to Use Manual Entry

- You want to set an **exact wording** for a response to a specific question
- The information **is not found** in documents, on your website, or on Instagram
- You want to add **internal team knowledge** that is not documented anywhere
- You need to **correct** the agent's response to a particular question

<Callout title="Tip">
  Q&A pairs are the most precise way to control the agent's responses. Use them for your most important and frequently asked customer questions.
</Callout>
