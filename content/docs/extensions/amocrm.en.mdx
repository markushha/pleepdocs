The AmoCRM integration allows your AI agent to automatically create leads, sync contacts, and manage deals, providing seamless integration with your sales pipeline.

## Features

- **Lead sync** — the AI agent automatically creates leads in AmoCRM from customer conversations.
- **Contact field mapping** — customer contact data is automatically mapped to CRM fields and saved.
- **Pipeline and deal management** — the agent works with sales pipelines and moves deals through stages.
- **Custom field rules** — you define which custom fields are filled automatically based on conversation data.
- **Status automation** — lead and deal statuses are updated automatically based on specific actions in the chat.
- **Lead creation from chats** — a new lead is created in the CRM when a customer starts a conversation or shows interest in a product.

## How to connect

1. Go to the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard.
2. Find **AmoCRM** and click **Connect**.
3. Enter your AmoCRM account address and sign in.
4. Grant the required data access permissions.
5. Configure contact field mapping — specify which conversation fields correspond to AmoCRM fields.
6. Select the pipeline and stage for new leads.
7. Set up status automation rules as needed.
8. Save your settings.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do operator responses trigger automations in AmoCRM?

No, operator responses do not trigger automations in AmoCRM. Automations are only triggered by AI agent actions. This is intentional to avoid duplicate actions and unexpected triggers during manual conversation management.
