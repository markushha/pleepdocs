The MySklad integration allows your AI agent to work with the product catalog, check inventory levels, and process orders directly during a conversation with a customer.

## Features

- **Product catalog sync** — the AI agent retrieves the up-to-date product catalog with names, descriptions, and specifications from MySklad.
- **Inventory management** — the agent checks product availability in the warehouse and provides customers with current stock information.
- **Order processing** — the AI agent can create orders in MySklad based on customer requests during the conversation.
- **Current pricing** — the agent provides customers with up-to-date prices from the system.

## How to connect

1. Go to the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard.
2. Find **MySklad** and click **Connect**.
3. Enter your MySklad account credentials and sign in.
4. Grant the required data access permissions.
5. Select the warehouses and product groups the AI agent will have access to.
6. Save your settings.

Once connected, the AI agent will be able to check product availability, provide current pricing, and process orders in real time.
