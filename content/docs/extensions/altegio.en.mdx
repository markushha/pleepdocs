The Altegio integration allows your AI agent to check schedules, find available slots, and book customers for services directly during a conversation.

## Features

- **Appointment booking** — the AI agent creates customer bookings for services by request during the conversation.
- **Schedule integration** — the agent retrieves up-to-date schedule information from Altegio.
- **Time slot management** — checks available slots accounting for service duration and specialist workload.
- **Specialist selection** — the customer can choose a specific specialist or be booked with the nearest available one.

## How to connect

1. Go to the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard.
2. Find **Altegio** and click **Connect**.
3. Enter your Altegio account credentials and sign in.
4. Grant the required permissions.
5. Select the branches and services available for online booking through the AI agent.
6. Save your settings.

Once connected, the AI agent will be able to work with Altegio schedules in real time, offering customers available slots and creating bookings.
