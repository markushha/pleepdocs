The Bitrix24 integration allows your AI agent to work with your CRM system: sync contacts, manage deals, and track interactions in the timeline.

## Features

- **CRM connection** — the AI agent connects directly to your Bitrix24 portal and works with data in real time.
- **Contact sync** — customer contact data is automatically created and updated in the CRM.
- **Deal management** — the agent creates and updates deals based on customer actions during conversations.
- **Timeline tracking** — all customer interactions through the AI agent are logged in the CRM timeline, providing a complete communication history.

## How to connect

1. Go to the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard.
2. Find **Bitrix24** and click **Connect**.
3. Enter your Bitrix24 portal address and sign in.
4. Grant the required data access permissions.
5. Configure the pipeline and stage for new deals.
6. Save your settings.

## Frequently asked questions

### Do operator responses trigger automations in Bitrix24?

No, operator responses do not trigger automations in Bitrix24. Automations are only triggered by AI agent actions. This is intentional to avoid duplicate actions and unexpected triggers during manual conversation management.
