The Google Calendar integration allows your AI agent to check available time slots and create events directly during a conversation with a customer.

## Features

- **Availability checking** — the AI agent sees free slots in your calendar and offers them to customers.
- **Event creation from conversations** — the agent creates calendar events at the customer's request during the chat.
- **Automatic reminders** — customers receive standard Google Calendar notifications.

## How to connect

Connection is done by logging into your account:

1. Go to the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard.
2. Find **Google Calendar** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to your Google account and grant the required permissions.
4. Select the calendar to use for bookings.
5. Save your settings.

Once connected, the AI agent will be able to work with your calendar in real time.

## Frequently asked questions

### Are holidays automatically accounted for?

No, holidays are not auto-detected. If you do not work on holidays, create events in your calendar on those dates to block the corresponding slots. This ensures the AI agent will not offer customers appointments on non-working days.
