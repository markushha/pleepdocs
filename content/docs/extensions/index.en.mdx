Extensions let you connect external services and business tools to Pleep, significantly expanding your AI agent's capabilities.

## What do extensions offer?

Without extensions, the AI agent answers customer questions using information from the knowledge base. With extensions, it can:

- **Work with CRM** — automatically create leads, sync contacts, and manage deals in AmoCRM or Bitrix24.
- **Manage appointments** — check available slots and book customers via Google Calendar or Altegio.
- **Handle products and orders** — retrieve up-to-date stock, pricing, and process orders from MySklad.

## Available extensions

| Extension | Purpose |
|---|---|
| [Google Calendar](/docs/extensions/google-calendar) | Availability checks, event creation from conversations |
| [AmoCRM](/docs/extensions/amocrm) | Leads, contacts, pipelines, deals, status automation |
| [Bitrix24](/docs/extensions/bitrix24) | CRM connection, contacts, deals, timeline tracking |
| [Altegio](/docs/extensions/altegio) | Appointment booking, schedule integration, slot management |
| [MySklad](/docs/extensions/mysklad) | Product catalog, inventory, order processing |

## How to connect an extension

Each extension is connected from the **Extensions** section in the Pleep dashboard. The connection process varies by service — detailed instructions are available on each extension's page.
