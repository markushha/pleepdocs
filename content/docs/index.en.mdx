Pleep is not another chatbot. It's an AI agent that qualifies leads, handles objections, and closes deals — across text and voice — before your team even wakes up.

## Get started in 5 minutes

1. Paste your website or Instagram URL
2. Pleep analyzes your business and generates a sales script
3. Connect a messenger — and the AI agent starts selling
