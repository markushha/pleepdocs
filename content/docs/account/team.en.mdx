Pleep supports team collaboration with a flexible system of roles and access control.

## Team roles

Pleep provides three roles with different access levels:

### Owner

- Full access to all features and settings.
- Subscription and payment management.
- Adding and removing team members.
- Assigning roles to other members.
- Account deletion.

### Admin

- AI agent settings management.
- Access to analytics and reports.
- Knowledge base management.
- Viewing and participating in conversations.
- Inviting new members (with the Member role).

### Member

- Viewing customer conversations.
- Participating in conversations as an operator.
- Access to basic analytics.

## Inviting members

To invite a new team member:

1. Go to **/me** > **Team**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the invitee's email address.
4. Select a role (Admin or Member).
5. Click **Send Invitation**.

The invitee will receive an email with a link to join your team.

## Managing members

In the **Team** section, you can:

- **Change a role** — click on the member's name and select a new role.
- **Remove a member** — click the delete button next to the name.
- **Revoke an invitation** — cancel a pending invitation before it is accepted.
- **Resend an invitation** — if the member did not receive or notice the email.

## Pending invitations

The team section displays all pending invitations with their status. You can revoke an invitation at any time before it is accepted.

## Limitations

- A team can have only one Owner.
- Owner role transfer is available in account settings.
- The number of members depends on your plan.
