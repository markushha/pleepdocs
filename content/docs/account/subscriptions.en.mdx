Pleep offers three plans designed for different business needs. This section explains how to manage your subscription.

## Subscription plans

### Light — $81.50/mo

- 1,500 messages/mo
- 1 messenger
- Knowledge base and monitoring
- Website widget
- Detailed analytics
- Working hours
- Technical support

### Business — $131.50/mo

- Everything in Light
- 1,500 messages/mo (base)
- 3 messengers
- CRM integrations (AmoCRM, Bitrix24)
- Google Calendar and Altegio
- Follow-ups
- WhatsApp broadcasts

### Pro — $251.50/mo

- Everything in Business
- Unlimited channels
- Custom API integrations
- Early access to new features
- Dedicated support

<Callout title="Note">
  Starting prices shown for 1,500 messages/mo. Price scales with volume.
</Callout>

## Viewing your current plan

1. Go to **/me** > **Subscription**.
2. You will see your current plan, next payment date, and available message quota.

## Changing your plan

You can switch to a different plan at any time:

1. Open the **Subscription** section.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select the plan that suits your needs.
4. Confirm the change.

When upgrading, the cost difference is calculated proportionally for the remaining period. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Managing payments

In the subscription section, you can:

- View payment history.
- Update payment details.
- Download receipts and invoices.

## Message quota

In the quota section, you can see:

- **Current usage** — how many messages have been used this month.
- **Monthly limit** — the maximum number of messages on your plan.
- **Purchase additional messages** — if the limit is exhausted, you can buy more messages without changing your plan.

## Canceling your subscription

If you decide to cancel:

1. Go to **Subscription** > **Manage Subscription**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Optionally provide a reason for cancellation.
4. Confirm your decision.

After cancellation, your account remains active until the end of the current billing period.
