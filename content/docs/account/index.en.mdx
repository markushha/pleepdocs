The account management section contains all settings related to your Pleep account. Access settings via the **/me** page.

## What you can configure

- **Profile** — change email, password, and personal preferences.
- **Subscriptions** — view current plan, upgrade/downgrade, billing history.
- **Team** — invite members, manage roles and access, revoke invitations.
- **Referral program** — generate referral link, track bonuses and referred users.
- **Promo codes** — apply promo codes to activate discounts.
- **Notifications** — configure email alert preferences and notification types.
- **Message quota** — current message usage, monthly limits, purchase additional messages.

## Subscription plans

Pleep offers three subscription tiers:

| Plan | Description |
|---|---|
| Light | Base plan with 1 messenger, analytics, and widget |
| Business | Extended plan with CRM integrations and broadcasts |
| Pro | Full-featured plan with unlimited channels and dedicated support |

Platform features are gated by plan level.

## Accessing settings

To access account settings, click the profile icon in the top-right corner of the dashboard or navigate directly to **/me**.
