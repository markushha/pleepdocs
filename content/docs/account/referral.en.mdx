The Pleep Referral Program lets you and your friends earn bonus messages. Invite a friend, and each of you receives **1,500 bonus messages**.

## How it works

1. You generate a unique referral link.
2. Your friend signs up for Pleep using your link.
3. Once your friend activates a subscription, you both receive 1,500 bonus messages.

## Where to find your referral link

1. Go to **/me** > **Referral Program**.
2. Click **Generate Link** (if a link has not been created yet) or copy the existing one.
3. Share it however you like — via messenger, email, or social media.

## Tracking bonuses

In the referral program section, you can see:

- **Referred users list** — who signed up using your link.
- **Status of each referral** — pending activation or activated.
- **Total bonus messages earned** — cumulative bonus from all referrals.

## Program terms

- Bonus messages are credited after the invited user activates a paid subscription.
- There is no limit on the number of invitations — invite as many friends as you want.
- Bonus messages are added to your current message limit.
- Bonus messages remain valid for the duration of your subscription.
