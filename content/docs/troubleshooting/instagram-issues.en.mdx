This section covers the most common Instagram issues and how to resolve them.

## Connection problems

### Instagram won't connect

If you get an error when connecting Instagram:

1. Make sure your Instagram account is a **Business account** or **Creator account**. Personal accounts are not supported.
2. Verify that your Instagram account is linked to a **Facebook Page**. This is a mandatory requirement of the Instagram API.
3. Go to **Channels** > **Instagram** and click **Reconnect**.
4. Authorize via Facebook and grant all requested permissions.

### How to switch to a business account

If you have a personal Instagram account:

1. Open Instagram > **Settings** > **Account**.
2. Select **Switch to Professional Account**.
3. Choose **Business**.
4. Link to a Facebook Page.

### Connection drops intermittently

- Check that you have not revoked permissions in Instagram or Facebook settings.
- Make sure your Facebook account password has not changed.
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the channel.

## Permission issues

### Insufficient permissions

Pleep requires the following permissions to work correctly:

- **Page messages management** — to read and send messages in Direct.
- **Instagram account access** — to retrieve profile information.
- **Comment management** — to reply to comments (if enabled).

To update permissions:

1. Go to Facebook settings > **Business Integrations**.
2. Find the Pleep application.
3. Click **View and Edit** and ensure all required permissions are granted.
4. Return to Pleep and reconnect the channel.

### Permissions were revoked

If you accidentally revoked permissions:

1. Go to **Channels** > **Instagram**.
2. Click **Reconnect**.
3. When authorizing via Facebook, make sure to grant all requested permissions.

## Messages not appearing in Pleep

If customers are writing in Direct but messages do not appear in Pleep:

1. **Check connection status** — make sure the Instagram channel is active under **Channels**.
2. **Reconnect the channel** — sometimes reconnecting resolves the issue.
3. **Check for restrictions** — Instagram may limit API access if it suspects policy violations.

## Instagram API limitations

Keep the following limitations in mind:

- Pleep can only respond to messages received within the last **24 hours** (Instagram limitation).
- Some media types may not be supported.
- Story stickers and reactions are processed as text messages.

If the issue remains unresolved, contact [Pleep support](/docs/troubleshooting).
