This section covers the most common WhatsApp issues and step-by-step instructions for resolving them.

## Connection problems

### Connection lost

If WhatsApp shows a "Disconnected" status:

1. Go to **Channels** > **WhatsApp**.
2. Click **Reconnect**.
3. Follow the instructions to re-authorize via WhatsApp Business API (WABA).
4. Wait for the connection confirmation.

### WABA setup issues

If you are connecting WhatsApp Business API for the first time:

1. Make sure you have a verified Facebook business account (Meta Business).
2. Verify that the phone number is not linked to a regular WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app.
3. Follow the step-by-step instructions in the WhatsApp connection section.
4. If you encounter a verification error, contact Pleep support.

## Ban recovery

If your number has been blocked by WhatsApp:

1. **Identify the reason** — WhatsApp typically sends a notification explaining the cause.
2. **Submit an appeal** — open WhatsApp on your phone and follow the instructions to file an appeal.
3. **Review your message content** — make sure your messages comply with WhatsApp's policies.
4. **Wait for a response** — appeal review can take anywhere from 24 hours to several days.

### How to avoid bans

- Do not send bulk messages without recipient consent.
- Use WhatsApp-approved message templates.
- Respond to user complaints promptly.
- Maintain a high quality rating.

## Quality rating drops

WhatsApp evaluates your account quality. If your rating drops:

1. **Check your rating** — go to **Channels** > **WhatsApp** > **Quality**.
2. **Analyze the causes** — common reasons include:
   - Users marking your messages as spam.
   - Users blocking your number.
   - Sending irrelevant content.
3. **Take action**:
   - Improve message relevance.
   - Make sure the AI agent responds on-topic.
   - Reduce the frequency of outbound messages.

## Message delivery failures

### Messages not delivered

- Check the WhatsApp connection status.
- Verify that the recipient's number is correct and active.
- Check whether you have exceeded your plan's message limit.
- If using templates, make sure they are approved.

### Messages delivered with delay

- Check the stability of your internet connection.
- Verify that the Pleep server is operating normally.
- Minor delays during high load are normal.

If the problem persists, contact [Pleep support](/docs/troubleshooting).
