This section contains solutions to the most common issues you may encounter while using Pleep.

## General recommendations

Before contacting support, try these steps:

1. **Refresh the page** — sometimes a simple browser refresh resolves the issue.
2. **Check your internet connection** — make sure your connection is stable.
3. **Clear browser cache** — outdated data can cause errors.
4. **Check service status** — verify that the platform is operating normally.

## Contact support

If you did not find a solution in this section, reach out to our support team:

- **Support chat** — click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the dashboard.
- **Email** — write to support@pleep.app.
- **Telegram** — contact @pleep_support.

When reaching out, please include:

- A description of the problem.
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible).
- Which channel is affected (WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, etc.).
- When the issue occurred.
